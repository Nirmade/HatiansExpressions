Explore diverse collections of arts at our gallery and gift shop We offer a wide range of artwork catering both to collectors with big budgets and art enthusiasts with smaller pockets.

I made my site easy to navigate and to properly focus on what you might want to visit. The page paintings show my artwork through a sphere of interest: Portraits, Nature, Cartoons, and Sketches. The page Books presents a summary of my books and then contains four subpages: Fiction, texts of a philosophical nature, Poetry, Comics, and Story time; there, all the stories are written in the first person point of view, a style I like but not all the stories are instances of my life, this page is twinning with Story (page) in the Films category. Yes, I have a section film, which for now, has short videos of my art and voice-over songs; singing, a hobby of mine, and an audio story (page) for those who want to hear my voice.
